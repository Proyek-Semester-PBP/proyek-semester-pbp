Features

PlasTIX Profile

By registering for a PlasTIX account, you will be granted access to all our available features! Customize your profile and check on your points count on our dedicated profile page.

Recycle

Got any plastic waste to throw away? If so, then head on over to one of our recycling posts! We've provided a list of locations where you can turn in your trash for points at our site. We also provide a delivery service for those who live far away.

Redeem

Once you've collected enough points from trading in your plastic waste, you can redeem those points for various items such as store coupons and phone credit.

Shopping

Want to get into the eco-friendly lifestyle? Our shopping page showcases products and vendors with a focus on recycled materials. Can't afford an item right now? You can make bookmarks and check back later.

News

Looking for a good read? We bring you all the latest articles on Sustainable Energy Transition and other environmental topics from various sources.
